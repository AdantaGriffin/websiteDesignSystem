Colors

There are many different color options and a four simple ways to applied them. They ways I learned so far are:

  1. Named colors: English words that describe color.

    • Blue
    • Green
    • Brown

  2. Hexadecimal: Hex colors begin with a hash "#" and are followed by 3 or 6 numerical/alphabetical characters.

    • #f08080
    • #d7e7d8
    • #bada55

  3. RGB: (Red, Blue, Green)Uses decimals rather than hexadecimals.

    • rgb(232, 41, 41)
    • rgb(155, 235, 229)
    • rgb(7, 38, 198)

  4. Hue Saturation Lightness: Hue is the first number, it refers to an angle on a color wheel. Saturation refers to the intentsity or purity of the color. Lightness refers to how light or dark the color is.

    • rgb(123, 72, 83)
    • rgb(24, 90, 80)
    • rgb(300, 80, 80)

Fonts

Typography is the art of arranging text on a webpage. Text appearance can be arranged in many different ways using CSS.

  1. Font-family: Changes the typeface of text on a webpage
    (font-family: Helvetica;).
  2. Font-size: Changes the size of text on a webpage
    (font-size: 1.2em;).
  3. Font-weight: Controls how bold or thin font appears
    font-weight: bold;)
  4. Text-align: Aligns the element that holds text
    (text-align: center;)
